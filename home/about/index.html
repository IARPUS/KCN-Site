This is USC Korean Culture Night

We are a student-run organization at University Southern California campus that presents an annual production to celebrate Korean and Korean-American culture through the integration of artistic elements like theatre, music, and dance.

Our rich Korean culture – our diverse and delicious food, our rising K-Pop culture, our deep history, our advancements with technology – is what our staff is dedicated to spreading and sharing with the USC and Los Angeles community.

Since the first Korean Culture Night held at USC in 2012, we have consistently invited not only the USC community to our production, but also the surrounding local Los Angeles community to experience Korean and Korean-American culture.

What each of us do

We like to divide the club tasks into two main groups: the logistics and the creative aspects of our production.

Producers

Producers are those who oversee all the logistical workings of the club and work together with the directors to build their vision of the show.

Directors

Directors are those who oversee all the creative workings of the club and work together with the producers to build their vision of the show.

Finance

  • Budgets the production and keep records of the inflow & outflow of funds
  • In charge of fundraisers and any other work dealing with money
  • Key holders of the KCN bank account and the use of the club’s visa card, checks, and cash
  • Organization of APASA and venue ordeals

Performing Arts

  • Responsible for all things related to performing arts such as band, dance, and etc...
  • Works closely with media productions and public relations to produce any needed videos or multimedia projects
  • Create and explore creative ways to promote KCN as a club

Public Relations

  • Responsible for shaping the face of KCN in all public affairs (social media, press, promotional events)
  • Collaborates with campus organizations & outside organizations for even more effective promotion

Media Production

  • Films KCN trailers, the needed segments of the show, as well as advertisement videos
  • Works closely with the director, and public relations to produce any needed videos or multimedia projects
  • Upload recap videos of the production show to KCN's official Youtube

Internal Relations

  • Organizes staff social events
  • Supporting Finance team with on-campus fundraisers ideas and event planning and setups
  • Supporting the Performing Arts team with planning and execution

Graphic Design

  • Creators of a visually appealing and graphically unified KCN by representing and portraying the visual of the production

Operations

  • Responsible for all required logistics pre-show and post-show
    • Works closely with producers to attend to all deadlines and required documents
    • Fulfill and manage all stage-related tasks
