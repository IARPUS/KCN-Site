Most Recent Shows

2018-2019: Gireogi Dad



This year is especially exciting and significant because we aimed to use Korean Culture Night as an opportunity to highlight the struggles that immigrants face on a daily basis when moving from Korea to America. The production focused on gireogi appa(s), which is defined as fathers who stay in Korea to financially support their children who study abroad. Our motives behind addressing these immigration stories was to bring awareness to the hardships that both the children and parents face.

2016-2017: Jaebi Sikdang



This year, a crucial theme of our show is one where the characters are able to explore the true meaning of friendship, family unity, and romance, even amidst the real life struggles of bankruptcy and social stance. In addition, this year's production includes various elements from many different Korean dramas, in order to incorporate the contemporary culture of South Korea.
